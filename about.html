About Me

As a graduate of the 2019 UC Davis Coding Boot Camp, I am a full stack web developer. My experience extends to both front-end and back-end web development.

As a web developer, I enjoy learning new programming languages and I look forward to developing new web based and mobile applications. I am also well versed in developing new training modules via online platforms, using new software to create interactive training support. When I am not consumed with coding, I spend my free time traveling the world and hiking with my dogs.
