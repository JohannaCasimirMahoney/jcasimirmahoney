About Me
I am Johanna (Jo) Casimir-Mahoney. A 2014 graduate of California State University, Monterey Bay, with a Master's Degree in Instructional Science and Technology. As a graduate of the 2019 UC Davis Coding Boot Camp, I am a full stack web developer. My experience extends to both front-end and back-end web development.

As a web developer, I enjoy learning new programming languages and I look forward to developing new web based and mobile applications. I am also well versed in developing new training modules via online platforms, using new software to create interactive training support.
